Our Aim

Educive Foundation aims to make AI education accessible to students from underprivileged backgrounds by:

  • Foundational Training: Workshops on Python, data literacy and ML basics.
  • Mentorship: One-to-one mentorship and group projects with volunteers.
  • Research Pathways: Bridge students to internships, research labs and open projects.
  • Resources: Free learning materials, recorded sessions and curated reading lists.

Short-term targets (6–12 months)

  1. Run 8 beginner workshops (online and in-person)
  2. Mentor 30 students on capstone projects
  3. Establish partnerships with 3 research labs for internship slots

Long-term vision

To create a self-sustaining community where motivated students from any background can learn AI, contribute to research, and access careers in technology.
